Salary of Comicbook Makers

Entertainment is a important aspect of peoples lives and awhile back, one of the best sources of entertainment was comics

Sean Jordan, founder of Army Ant Publishing, said that freelance comic book artists were paid anywhere from about $220 to $4,400 per book project, which breaks down to $10 to $200 per page. artists that use pencil or ink to draw can ask for $75 to $200 a page. In 2010, illustrators, along with other fine artists, had an average annual salary of around $53,000. You don't really need any eduction for this job, you just have to be able to draw or tell a story well.
